About App

Behind of making this application.

Any idea comes with a problem statement,This app sloves the problem that occured in our campus by connecting voluntary donors to receivers. Voluntary donors may be you, your firends or any other person.

If a person requires a blood then he can make a request of blood type, units of blood with his details, The apps pins his location with Red Color location and with necessary details in the Google Maps and sends notifications to all users. All registered persons using the app can see the request and can respond to you by mobile phone.

This app also had a feature of donate blood. If you are willing to donate blood, you can also make a request in the app which pins with green color location.
